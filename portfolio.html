Portfolio Blog Resume

Burger Eater | Demo

An interactive menu that allows you to order a new burger, choosing whether or not the burger will be for here or to go. Once you are done eating, you can take your remainder to go! This app remembers your orders and allows you to order time and time again.

Simple Game | Demo

A math based guessing game that provides you with four buttons whose values are unknown. You win the game by clicking the buttons until the goal number is reached.

Facebook API | Demo

A space to compare two Facebook pages of two different organizations. In order to better online presence, this application seeks to build an understanding about social media best practices and gain lasting impressions in their online presence. I worked primarily on understanding and implementing the Facebook API aspect of this project.

Nap Survey | Demo

A survey that tells you how long you should nap based on the answers to your tired-ness related questions. Bear with the minimalistic design and recieve a cute picture of a cat as your reward!
